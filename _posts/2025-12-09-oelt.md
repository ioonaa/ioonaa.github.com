The <strong>49th Austrian Linguistics Conference</strong> took place at the University of Klagenfurt and featured
the phonetics workshop <em>„Laute von allen Seiten: Phonetik und ihre Teilbereiche als transdiszplinäres Interessensfeld“</em>
where we presented and discussed our work-in-progress on question intonation in Bulgarian Judeo-Spanish.
We thank the organizers <strong>Nathalie Elsässer</strong>, <strong>Hendrik Behrens-Zemek</strong>, <strong></strong> (ISF Austrian Academy of Sciences), and <strong>Lukas Nemestothy</strong> (University of Vienna) for putting together a highly varied program and for fostering a very pleasant atmosphere of exchange.

![Foto]({{ site.url }}/images/oelt.jpg "ÖLT 2025 impressions"){:height="300" .center-image}

I would like to highlight the plenary talk by <strong>Brigitta Busch</strong> (University of Vienna) entitled <em>Hinhören! Poslušajmo. Slowenisch sprechen in Kärnten. Govoriti slovensko na Koroškem</em> which gave a brilliant historical overview of multilingualism in the Kärnten region.
